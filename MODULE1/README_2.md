# Baking Cookies Algorithm for Multiple Batches

```mermaid
graph TD
    A[Start] --> B[Determine Number of Batches]
    B --> C[Get 4 cups of flour per batch]
    C --> D[Get 1/3 cup of sugar per batch]
    D --> E[Get 1/5 cup of butter per batch]
    E --> F[Get 1/4 Teaspoon baking powder per batch]
    F --> G[Get 1 egg per batch]
    G --> H[Get 2 Teaspoons of milk per batch]
    H --> I[Mix Ingredients]
    I --> J[Preheat oven to 350 degrees]
    J --> K[Put Cookies on the Baking Sheet]
    K --> L[Bake Cookies for 15 minutes]
    L --> M[Cool Cookies]
    M --> N[Serve]
    N --> O[End]
